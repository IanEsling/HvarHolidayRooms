Jana has taught yoga full-time since 1999, based from London, England and leading workshops and retreats throughout Europe.

She teaches throughout the summer from her studio in Dol running workshops and classes for all ages and abilities, ranging from complete beginners to more experienced and advanced students looking to deepen and challenge their existing practice.

More details of Jana's schedule of workshops and retreats in Dol, London and elsewhere in Europe, as well as more information about Shadow Yoga can be found on her website at Yoga In The City.
