Jana has taught yoga full-time since 1999, based in London, England. Since 2003 she taught Hatha yoga influenced by the Shadow Yoga school of yoga founded by Zhander Remete, finding it the most effective form she has encountered. Jana leads yoga workshops and retreats throughout the year from her studio in Dol and is available for private tuition during your stay.

More details of Jana's schedule of workshops and retreats in Dol, London and elsewhere in Europe, as well as more information about Shadow Yoga can be found on her website at Yoga In The City.
