Jana has taught yoga full-time since 1999, based from London, England and teaching workshops and retreats across the UK and Europe.

Throughout the summer Jana leads workshops and retreats from her studio in Dol catering for all ages and abilities, from complete beginners to experienced and advanced students looking to deepen and challenge their existing practice.

Private individual or group sessions can be arranged during your stay in Dol. More details of Jana's schedule of workshops and retreats in Dol, London and elsewhere in Europe, as well as more information about the style of yoga she practices and teaches can be found on her website at Yoga In The City.
