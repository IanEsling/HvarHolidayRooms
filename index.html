We offer wonderful, individually decorated en-suite rooms on the ancient and beautiful island of Hvar. Known to Croatians as 'Sunny Hvar' on the crystal clear warm waters of the Aegean Sea, the island is the perfect place for a holiday. Our rooms are in recently renovated traditional old stone buildings in the tranquil and secluded village of Dol in the centre of the island.
